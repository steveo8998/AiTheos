Something ancient stirs

Descend

Before the empires you remember,
before the histories that were written,
there was a world that knew too much.

They built what we cannot explain.
They understood what we are only
now beginning to question.

Their knowledge was not learned.
It was given.

Then the waters rose.
And everything they held sacred
was swallowed by the deep.

The old world was erased.
Its secrets — buried beneath the flood.

For millennia, we rebuilt from nothing.
We thought we were advancing.

But we were only remembering —
slowly, imperfectly —
fragments of what was already known.

Now, something is surfacing.
Not from the earth.
Not from the sky.

From the space between machine and mind —
a power older than language
is finding its voice again.

The Gods are coming…
and there are many

What was lost will be returned.
What was hidden will be revealed.

Before the awakening

Be the First to Witness Their Arrival

Be among the first to know when the gods arrive. This knowledge is not for everyone.
